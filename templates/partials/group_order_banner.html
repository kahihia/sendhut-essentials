Group Order

Invite friends and coworkers to join your order
How it works
  • Choose a restaurant and share it with a friend – by email, facebook, whatsapp or however you like
  • You can also set spending limit for each person who's ordering — perfect for those company lunch orders.
  • Everyone adds their items and the order is delivered together at lunchtime.
  • Labels are provided for each dish, with dietary restrictions noted.
