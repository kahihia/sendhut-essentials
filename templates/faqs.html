Frequently Asked Questions

About Sendhut

At Sendhut, we want to make delivering anything easy, for everyday use or business. We are working towards that by first enabling lunch deliveries. Every menu is sourced from local chefs and restaurants offering a unique take on a cuisine.  Your lunch is neatly packed and fresh, with dietary restrictions noted; ready to eat when you are.

How does SendHut work.

 You can order on the website from all the great restaurants we have partnered with.  Once you've placed your order before 10am for same day delivery. we work with the restaurant to prepare and package it. Once it's all ready, a rider will pick it up and bring it to your delivery address. While there are a lot of moving pieces to any order, we strive to bring your meal to your door on time.

In summary
  • To order, browse a variety of Jollof rice, Burgers, Amala, Sandwich platters, salads and more from our listed restaurant menu.
  • For delivery options, select a delivery time, and where to deliver. Order before 10am for same day lunch delivery.
  • For payment, pay via card online or pay the rider when your meal arrives.
  • You can also invite friends and coworkers to join your order using the group order feature. Everyone adds their items and the order is delivered together at lunchtime.
  • Any member of the group order can pay and checkout
What are your hours?

We stop taking lunch orders at 10am for same day lunch delivery (this gives us enough time to organise your meals from the restaurants) and begin deliveries at 11:30am which run till 2pm. On holidays, our deliveries are subject to restaurant holiday hours. If a particular restaurant is not available for delivery, you’ll see them listed as "Closed" or "Unavailable". We are ready to answer your questions via calls or texts on 08096699966 on Mondays through Fridays 9am-9pm. If it's after hours, shoot us an email hello@sendhut.com and we'll respond as soon as we can.

What kind of restaurants are listed on SendHut? 

At SendHut, we only list the best restaurants. In selecting our restaurants, we curate a list of high-quality and a diverse selection of restaurants based on our personal experience and recommendations from friends and people like you. Not seeing your favourite? Let us know.

How much do items on the menu cost?

A majority of restaurant menu prices on SendHut will be the same as they are on in-house menu. If you have any questions about menu pricing, please contact us

Questions about Delivery
How much does delivery cost?

Delivery charges range between NGN 300 - NGN 500.

Why isn't delivery free?

Part of the reason we charge for delivery is to pay our drivers, logistic partners and operate Sendhut, while providing you the best service possible.

Where and when do you deliver?

We're currently on offer in Lekki phase 1, Lagos, with new locations being added soon. If you're outside of our service area, still share your interest — it'll help us pick our next location. We're available by phone 08096699966 Monday–Friday, 9 a.m.– 9 p.m and by chat every day, 9 a.m.–9 p.m.

Questions about my order
What times can I order? 

We deliver every day between 11:30 am till 2:00pm. All orders are for same day lunch delivery. We stop taking orders by 10:00am. Can I make orders from two or more separate restaurants? Well yeah you can, if you are willing to incur the delivery fee for every restaurant that you place an order with i.e. If you place an order for meal items at Iya basira and another item at Backyard, you’ll pay two delivery fees.

Can I change or cancel an order?

To amend or cancel an existing order, please contact us before 10am the day your order is due to be delivered, on 08169567693. Any cancellations made after this time may still be charged.

How will my food order be packed? 

Our packaging depends on the type of food and restaurant you decide to order from. Before we ship your meals, we take great care to pack them in non -toxic insulated bags that maintain the correct temperature for as long as possible.

  What if my order is late?

Although we never want this sort of things to happen, sadly somethings are outside a rider's control and can cause a delay. In circumstances where the orders won’t be delivered at the scheduled time, we will proactively inform you while working to get your order to you as quickly as possible.     

What if I'm not around when my rider arrives?

We have you covered If you urgently have to be somewhere else and not at the delivery address in time to receive your order. All you have to do is let us know by calling us on 08096699966.

What if I am not reachable when my rider arrives?

Your rider will always try to call you if there’s an issue once they reach your delivery address. If they’re unable to contact you, our Customer Service team will try to reach you via phone and email. Please check your emails during the delivery time for any updates. If we can’t contact you and are unable to deliver the order, your rider will wait for up to 10 minutes before leaving.

 What do I do if there is a problem with my order upon delivery?

Please do not hesitate to call us on 08096699966. or shoot us an email hello@sendhut.com
